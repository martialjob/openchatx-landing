PurrrChat

No Ads. No Tracking. Just Chat.

🚀 An alternative SimpleX Client (α coming soon).

Why PurrrChat?

🔒 Secure & Private

End-to-end encryption ensures your conversations stay private.

🚀 Fast & Lightweight

PureChat is a PWA, installable on any device with no app store required.

🌍 Open & Decentralized

Built on SimpleX, ensuring no central servers track your activity.

Coming Soon

PurrrChat is still in development. Stay tuned for the upcoming α-release.
